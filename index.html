Regulaite

Ensuring a Future with Safe AI.

Facilitating effective AI regulation is one of the most critical challenges of our​​ era.

We have established Regulaite to address this challenge head-on, with a singular mission: to promote responsible AI governance worldwide.

Our name, Regulaite, reflects our commitment to guiding the development and deployment of AI in ways that prioritize humanity's well-being.

We work closely with international partners in Austria, Switzerland, and Belgrade, alongside our base in the United States, to create a comprehensive and globally relevant framework for AI regulation.

Our approach integrates safety and innovation, viewing them as interconnected challenges to be addressed through cutting-edge research and collaborative policy-making. We aim to advance AI capabilities responsibly, ensuring that ethical considerations are at the forefront of technological progress.

Our dedicated team is focused exclusively on this mission, free from the distractions of conventional corporate pressures. This allows us to remain agile, responsive, and dedicated to the long-term goal of safe and equitable AI.

Regulaite invites the world’s foremost experts in AI, law, and ethics to join us in this vital endeavor. Together, we can shape policies that safeguard the future while fostering innovation.

If you share our vision and have the expertise to contribute, we offer a unique opportunity to impact the most pressing issue of our time.

Join us in crafting a future where AI serves humanity responsibly.
